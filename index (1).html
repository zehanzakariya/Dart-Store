Our Story

Born From
The Streets

Dart Store started in a bedroom with a heat press machine and a dream. We saw streetwear going corporate — oversized fits becoming designer price tags, custom culture disappearing behind big brand walls.

We built Dart to flip that. Custom printed oversized tees at honest prices. Every design born from hip-hop culture, graffiti walls, and the raw energy of cities that never sleep.

No gatekeeping. No waiting lists. Just you, your design, and premium cotton that drops right to your door. This is streetwear for the people who make the streets worth wearing.
